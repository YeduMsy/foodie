Frequently Asked Questions

~ Do I need a reservation?

While we always welcome walk-ins, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially on weekends and holidays, to guarantee your table.

~ Do you have dietary options?

Yes, absolutely. Our menu clearly labels items that are Vegetarian (V), Vegan (VG), and Gluten-Free (GF) to accommodate everyone.

~ Is parking available?

Yes, we want your arrival to be stress-free. We offer complimentary parking located directly behind the building.

~ Do you offer takeout?

Yes, we do! You can enjoy our full menu from the comfort of your home. We are partnered with Zomato and Swiggy for delivery.
